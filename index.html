Doing our part


"Don't Give pitty, Give Help"

Nationwide, there is a rising trend in the percentage of unsheltered homeless with a total count of over 580,000 people in 2020. Some people believe being homeless is of ones own doing, but in reality problems as large as this are never that simple. In the population of homeless individuals you will find families that were struck by natural disaster, people who suffer from disability, and individuals that have fallen on hard times. Due to the enviorment people are subjected to when homeless its often not just as simple as getting back on your feet.
